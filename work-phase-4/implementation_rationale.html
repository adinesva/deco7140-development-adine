Implementation Rationale

🧩 Alignment with Client and User Needs

The Navigating Australia website was developed to support the Connect Communities Initiative (CCI) by helping international students, workers, and newcomers access trustworthy resources, community networks, and events across Australia. The design directly reflects the client's goals of belonging, inclusion, and empowerment.

  • Home Page: Introduces the aim for the web page, with clear calls to action that encourage exploration and engagement.
  • Resources: Provides practical guides (housing, banking, healthcare) directly addressing challenges identified in the A3 Design Proposal.
  • Events and Community: Create platform for connection and participation, aligning with CCI's aim of strengthening social cohesion.
  • Design Decisions: navy-blue color palette and friendly flat illustrations establish trust and credibility, while clear navigation supports a smooth behavioural flow for diverse users.

♿ Accessibility and Ethical Considerations

Accessibility and ethical principles were integrated throughout the implementation process, drawing from findings in my A2 Design Research on inclusive digital design.

  • Layouts were tested for clarity under different zoom levels and screen widths to maintain usability across devices.
  • Ethically, the content avoids bias and maintains cultural sensitivity, using inclusive imagery and language that represents diverse backgrounds.
  • Following the WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines, with adequate color contrast, scalable typography, and keyboard-friendly navigation.

💡 Variations from A3 Design Proposal

Several refinements were made during development while maintaining the project’s original intent:

  • Color Scheme: The primary color changed from teal to navy blue to strengthen trust and professionalism.
  • Content Adjustments: Some resource topics were condensed to maintain readability and avoid overwhelming first-time users. These variations were guided by feasibility, time constraints, while maintaining alignment with the project’s overall concept and goals.

⚙️ Challenges and Strategies

  • Responsive Design: Ensuring layout consistency across devices was initially difficult. This was managed by creating a flexible grid system and testing iteratively on multiple breakpoints.
  • Content Adjustments: Some resource topics were condensed to maintain readability and avoid overwhelming first-time users. These variations were guided by feasibility, time constraints, while maintaining alignment with the project’s overall concept and goals.
  • Accessibility Compliance: Initial contrast issues were identified and resolved by deepening color tones and adjusting text brightness.
